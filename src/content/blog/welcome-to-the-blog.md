If you've landed here, you probably already watched a video on the Garage Geek Guy YouTube channel and wondered, *"is there a place I can just read this?"*

Now there is.

## What this blog is

Every post on this site is a written companion to a video on the channel. The video is embedded right at the top, so you can:

- **Watch the build** as a video, the way it was filmed, or
- **Skim the write-up** for the schematic, code, and parts list, or
- **Do both** — watch with one eye while copying a snippet with the other.

Posts will lean toward the *useful* end of the spectrum: pinouts, code that actually compiles, the gotcha I hit at minute 14 that took an hour to debug, and links to the parts I used.

## What it isn't

It's not a replacement for the YouTube channel — that's where new builds drop first, and the videos are still the canonical record. Think of this as the cheat sheet you'd want next to the bench while following along.

## What's coming

Every existing video on the channel will get its own post here over time, working backward through the catalog. New videos will get a post around the time they drop.

If you're new, the easiest way to keep up is to [subscribe on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@garagegeekguy) — the blog is just a more permanent home for the same content.

Let's get to it.
